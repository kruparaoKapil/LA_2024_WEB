The company is offering to sell undivided, super built-up space of commercial office space (in square feet) on the specific floor, along with a proportionate undivided share in the land, on which the said building is constructed, mentioned in more detail in Schedule - 'C' on the following terms and conditions :

  • The Seller offers to sell a part of the balance undivided share (in square feet) of commercial space, belonging to it in Schedule - 'A' alongwith proportionate undivided share in the land on which the said building is built, at Rs 40,000/- per square foot.
  • The company is offering for sale, a minimum super built-up area of twenty square feet and multiples of five square feet thereafter. Application for a fraction of square foot is not accepted, for sale.
  • The purchaser is required to pay Rs 27,000/- per square foot of super built- up area, for reserving/buying undivided commercial space, on the required floor in the Schedule - 'A' property and pay the balance Rs 13,000/- per square foot (including Rs. 4,000/- per square foot towards cost of amenities etc.,.) within forty eight months from the date of paying Rs 27,000/- per square foot. This period of 48 months, is given to the purchaser to pay the final/balance sale consideration, to enable him/her to have sufficient time to fully satisfy himself / herself as to the actual growth in value, rentals etc.,. from the property now being sold.
  • Since the advance paid by the purchaser for purchase of this property, would be used by the company for its own business activities, till receipt of the final/balance agreed sale consideration and registration/transfer of the property in favour of the purchaser, the company has offered to pay interest at 01% per month, each month, on the amount of advance received from the purchaser at Rs 27,000/- per square foot, for purchase of the Schedule - 'C' property on the following terms :
  1. Interest will accrue from the date of realization of cheques/drafts/pay orders or transfer of amount to the advance account of the company.
  2. Interest ceases to accrue, after the 48 th month of receipt of advance money at Rs 27,000/- per square foot, for area to be purchased.
  3. TDS will be deducted from the amount of interest as per the provisions of the Income Tax Act. However, purchasers opting for payment of interest without deduction of tax may submit, applicable forms, as applicable.
  • The company has explained to the purchaser that the entire Schedule - 'A' property is being leased out, floor wise and any person desirous of purchasing built-up space for his/her own use, he/she will have to purchase not less than 25% of the entire floor in Schedule - 'A'property. Otherwise the purchaser can purchase or own undivided share of office space in the floor mentioned in Schedule - 'C', along with other purchasers/owners of built-up office space in the said floor of Schedule - 'A'property.
  • If the purchaser is not satisfied with the expected rent or appreciation in value etc.,. of the property now being purchased, at the end of forty eighth month of paying the advance amount for purchase of the property, the company agrees to refund without any cancellation charges, the entire advance sale consideration received by it, within 30 days after receipt in writing, of an application seeking repayment of advance sale consideration paid by him/her. However, if the purchaser seeks to cancel this sale transaction before 48 months of such payment, the company shall refund to the purchaser the advance sale consideration received by it, after collecting cancellation charges at Rs 100/- per square foot, plus 50% of the actual interest (including TDS) paid to the purchaser.
  • After payment of the final/balance sale consideration for purchase of ‘Schedule C’ property, the purchaser. has the sole option of giving the Schedule - 'C' property, on lease for a minimum period of 20 years to the company, at the higher of 90% of actual average rent received per sft, per month, from all tenants on the same floor OR monthly rent of Rs 225/- per square foot, with an escalation of 05% every five years, on the previous monthly rent paid, from the end of the month of execution of lease deed in favor of the company.
